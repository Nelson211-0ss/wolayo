Our Why

Why Wolayo Matters

In Uganda, poverty and broken family systems are root causes of child homelessness, teenage pregnancies, and school dropouts. Wolayo provides a holistic approach—addressing not only material needs but also emotional healing and spiritual grounding.

By restoring hope and dignity, Wolayo is shaping a new generation of leaders who will break cycles of poverty for themselves and their communities.
